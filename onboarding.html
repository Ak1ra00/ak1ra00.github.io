Membership Onboarding

Welcome to the membership. This page sets expectations so your first 30 days are structured and measurable.

Week 1 Setup

  • Choose your training days and lock your weekly schedule.
  • Set your protein target and prep your first 3 days of meals.
  • Complete your baseline check-in and starting photos/metrics.

Weekly Flow

  • Monday: receive programming update and goals.
  • Mid-week: submit quick accountability check-in.
  • Weekend: review wins, misses, and next-week adjustments.

Support Expectations

  • Message response target: within 24 hours on weekdays.
  • Technique/form feedback: submitted videos reviewed weekly.
  • Escalation path: if pain spikes, regress and flag support immediately.
